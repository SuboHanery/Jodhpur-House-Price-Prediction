AI Engine Architecture

Technical overview of the machine learning model powering our real estate predictions.

Random Forest Regressor

Our prediction engine is powered by a Random Forest Regressor. This algorithm works by building a "forest" of multiple decision trees during training. When you request a prediction, each tree in the forest makes its own estimate based on different subsets of data.

The model then averages all these individual predictions together to produce the final estimated price. This "ensemble" approach prevents the AI from becoming too heavily biased by outlier data, ensuring a highly robust and accurate valuation for Jodhpur properties.

Key Features Analyzed

  • Location Encoding: One-hot encoding of 12 major Jodhpur localities.
  • Structural Data: Area Size, BHK, Bathrooms, and Balconies.
  • Quality of Life: 11 distinct premium amenities (Gym, Pool, Security, etc.).
  • Depreciation: Property age factored dynamically.
