Most Valuable Amenities

Our model indicates that properties with a Swimming Pool and Security see a 12-15% increase in base valuation. Gyms add an average of 8% to the property's estimated market price.

The Age Factor

Property depreciation in Jodhpur slows down significantly after 10 years. A new property commands a premium, but well-maintained 15-year-old properties in prime areas like Sardarpura retain up to 85% of their peak value.

BHK Sweet Spot

The highest demand and best ROI in the current market belongs to 3 BHK properties. They offer the perfect balance for families and show the most consistent month-over-month price appreciation.

Premium Localities

Shastri Nagar and Sardarpura consistently rank as the highest value-per-square-foot areas in our dataset, largely driven by proximity to top schools, hospitals, and commercial hubs.
